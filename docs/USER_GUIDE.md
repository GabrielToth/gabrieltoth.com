# Account Completion Flow - User Guide

## Welcome!

If you've logged in with Google, Facebook, or TikTok but haven't set a password yet, you'll need to complete your account setup. This guide will walk you through the process step by step.

## Why Complete Your Account?

Completing your account setup ensures that:

- Your account is secure with a password
- We have your contact information
- Your profile is fully set up and ready to use
- You can access all features of the platform

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Log In with OAuth

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Sign in with Google", "Sign in with Facebook", or "Sign in with TikTok"
3. Follow the OAuth provider's login process
4. You'll be redirected to the account completion page

### Step 2: Review Your Information

On the first step, you'll see your information from your OAuth provider:

- **Email:** Your email address
- **Name:** Your full name
- **Profile Picture:** Your profile picture from the OAuth provider

You can edit any of these fields if needed. Just click the "Edit" button next to each field.

**Important:** If you change your email, make sure it's not already registered with another account.

### Step 3: Add Required Information

On the second step, you'll need to provide:

#### Password

Your password must be strong and secure. It must contain:

- At least 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

**Example strong passwords:**

- `MySecure@Pass123`
- `Str0ng#Password`
- `P@ssw0rd2024`

**Tips for a strong password:**

- Use a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include numbers and special characters
- Avoid using common words or personal information
- Don't reuse passwords from other accounts
- Use a password manager to generate and store strong passwords

#### Phone Number

Enter your phone number in international format with a leading `+` sign.

**Format:** `+[country code][number]`

**Examples:**

- `+1 (555) 123-4567` (USA)
- `+55 11 9999-9999` (Brazil)
- `+34 91 234 5678` (Spain)
- `+49 123 456789` (Germany)

**Tips:**

- Include the country code
- You can include spaces, hyphens, or parentheses for readability
- The system will normalize the format

#### Birth Date

Enter your birth date in the format `YYYY-MM-DD`.

**Format:** `YYYY-MM-DD`

**Examples:**

- `1990-01-15`
- `1985-12-31`
- `2000-06-20`

**Requirements:**

- You must be at least 13 years old
- The date cannot be in the future
- Use the correct format

### Step 4: Verify Your Information

On the final step, review all your information:

- Your email address
- Your full name
- Your phone number
- Your birth date

If everything looks correct, click "Complete Account Setup". If you need to make changes, click "Edit" next to any section to go back and modify it.

## Troubleshooting

### "Your session has expired. Please log in again"

This message appears when your session has timed out. Sessions are valid for 30 minutes.

**Solution:** Start the login process again.

### "This email is already in use"

This message appears when the email address is already registered to another account.

**Solution:** Use a different email address or contact support if you believe this is an error.

### "Password does not meet security requirements"

This message appears when your password is not strong enough.

**Solution:** Make sure your password contains:
- At least 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

### "Please enter a valid international phone number"

This message appears when your phone number is not in the correct format.

**Solution:** Use the international format with a leading `+` sign (e.g., `+1234567890`).

### "You must be at least 13 years old"

This message appears when your birth date indicates you're under 13 years old.

**Solution:** Make sure you've entered the correct birth date.

### "Birth date cannot be in the future"

This message appears when your birth date is in the future.

**Solution:** Make sure you've entered the correct birth date.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I change my email after completing my account?

A: Yes, you can change your email in your account settings after completing the account setup.

### Q: Can I use the same email for multiple accounts?

A: No, each email address can only be registered to one account. If you need multiple accounts, use different email addresses.

### Q: What if I forget my password?

A: You can reset your password using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

### Q: Can I change my phone number or birth date later?

A: Yes, you can update your phone number and birth date in your account settings.

### Q: How long is my session valid?

A: Your session is valid for 30 minutes of inactivity. If you're inactive for more than 30 minutes, you'll need to log in again.

### Q: Is my information secure?

A: Yes, your information is encrypted and stored securely. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data.

### Q: Can I delete my account?

A: Yes, you can delete your account in your account settings. Please note that this action cannot be undone.

### Q: What if I have more questions?

A: Please contact our support team at support@gabrieltoth.com.

## Security Tips

### Protect Your Password

- Never share your password with anyone
- Don't use the same password on multiple websites
- Use a password manager to generate and store strong passwords
- Change your password regularly

### Protect Your Account

- Keep your email address secure
- Enable two-factor authentication if available
- Log out when using shared computers
- Be cautious of phishing emails

### Protect Your Personal Information

- Only provide information that's necessary
- Be cautious when sharing personal information online
- Review your privacy settings regularly
- Report suspicious activity to support
